Pragmatic Welcomes David Poole!

Pragmatic Consulting, Inc. is excited to announce the addition of David Poole to the organization as an Export, Import senior consultant.

He is an experienced and skilled former government investigator in International Trade and Arms Controls, as well as Counter Proliferation and Export Compliance. David has an exceptional record for gathering intelligence, investigating the export of military equipment, financial transactions, and other illegal activities. He is skilled in formulating plans, problem solving, resource organization and compliance enforcement.

With over 28 years of experience in federal law enforcement with the Department of Commerce Bureau of Industry and Security, U.S. Customs Service and Department of Justice, David has attained a knowledge and perspective that translates into solutions and value for his clients.

We are very pleased about David joining Pragmatic Consulting, Inc. as a Senior Consultant. His addition increases our capacity in Import/Export Compliance consulting and complements many facets of the Pragmatic team. We look forward to utilizing David's talents and expertise to provide our customers with an even higher level of excellence in these areas.
